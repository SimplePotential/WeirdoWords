WEIRDO WORDS
A Ludum Dare 51 entry — A word jumble game!
  1. You will be given a set of 10 semi-random words per day.
  2. You have 15 seconds to guess each word.
  3. Type the letters, drag/drop, or click tiles to place them.
  4. Backspace or click a placed letter to return it.
  5. Get 100 pts for correct, lose 20 pts for wrong.
  6. Earn 5 bonus pts for each second left on a correct guess.
  7. Underlined letters in a wrong guess are in the correct position.

Timed Daily is the only mode available.

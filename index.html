WEIRDO WORDS
A word jumble game where you have to guess the word before time runs out. The faster you guess, the more points you earn! But be careful, wrong guesses will cost you points and time!
  1. You will be given 10 words daily; they change at approximately Midnight local time.
  2. You have seconds to guess each word.
  3. Type, Drag, or Click/Tap letter to build the word.
  4. Use Backspace or Click/Tap to move placed letters back.
  5. Correct: + points. Wrong: - points.
  6. For each second left on the clock, you earn + bonus points.
  7. Wrong guesses underline letters in the correct position and add seconds (up to seconds total) when at least 1 letter is correct.
  8. Missing a word shows as wrong, in italics, and reveals the correct word when timer expires.
  9. A valid but unexpected word is marked valid and earns + points but does not progress game.

Timed Daily is the only mode available but more modes are planned for the future! Follow the GitHub repo to stay up to date on development and new releases!

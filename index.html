WEIRDO WORDS
A word jumble challenge with two ways to play: Timed Daily for a fixed 10-word run, and Infinite Play for nonstop word solving with strategy tools like Hint and Skip.
© Simple Potential | GitHub Source
How To Play
Timed Daily
  1. You get 10 words each day, rotating around local midnight.
  2. You have seconds per word.
  3. Type, Drag, or Click/Tap letters to build your answer.
  4. Use Backspace or Click/Tap to move placed letters back.
  5. Correct answers earn + points. Wrong answers cost - points.
  6. Each second left on the timer adds + bonus points.
  7. Wrong guesses that include at least one correct-position letter add seconds back, up to seconds total.
  8. When time expires, the word is marked missed, shown in italics, and the answer is revealed.
  9. Valid but non-target words are marked valid and earn + points, but do not advance the round.
Infinite Play
  1. Play continuously with no per-word timer and no fixed word limit.
  2. Words cycle through eligible lengths and reshuffle when the pool is exhausted.
  3. Correct answers earn + points and move to the next word.
  4. Wrong answers cost - points.
  5. Hint is available only in Infinite Play. It places one correct letter and costs - points each use.
  6. You get a limited hint pool per word based on word length.
  7. Skip Word is available only in Infinite Play. It marks the word as skipped and costs - points.
  8. Valid but non-target words earn + points, but do not advance the round.
  9. Use End Run anytime to finish and view your results.

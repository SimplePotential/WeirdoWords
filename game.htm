WEIRDO WORDS
A word jumble game where you have to guess the word before time runs out. The faster you guess, the more points you earn! But be careful, wrong guesses will cost you points and time!
  1. You will be given a set of 10 semi-random words. The words you get will change each day at approximately Midnight local time.
  2. You have seconds to guess the word before the game moves onto the next word in the list.
  3. The easiest way to play is to simply type the letters you want to place.
  4. You can also drag/drop the letters or simply click the letter to place it in the next available slot.
  5. Backspace or clicking on a letter in play will move the letter back to the tile list.
  6. You'll receive points for a correct guess and lose points for an incorrect guess.
  7. For each second left on the clock, you'll earn bonus points.
  8. All guesses will appear in the left side list.
  9. A correct guess is green with a check mark, a wrong guess is red with an X.
  10. Missing a word entirely will show as wrong but also be in italics and display what the word was.
  11. Wrong guess will have any letters in the correct position underlined and you'll gain seconds (up to seconds total) if at least 1 letter was correct.

Timed Daily is the only mode available but more modes are planned for the future! Follow the GitHub repo to stay up to date on development and new releases!
